Important People in Iron Man's Life

Pepper Potts: Tony Stark's personal assistant, confidant, and love interest. She eventually becomes the CEO of Stark Industries.

James "Rhodey" Rhodes: Tony Stark's best friend and military liaison. He also dons the War Machine armor to fight alongside Iron Man.

Happy Hogan: Tony Stark's loyal bodyguard and head of security. He is a close friend and one of the few people Tony trusts implicitly.
